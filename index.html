Frequently Asked Questions

E-Waste management regulations and disposal methods.

  • What is E-waste management?

    E-waste management is a process to collect e-waste, recover and recycle material by safe methods, dispose of e-waste by suitable techniques

  • How do I practice e-waste management at home?

    1. Consider reducing the number of tech devices you own.
    2. Extend the life of your electronics.
    3. Buy environmentally friendly electronics.
    4. Donate used electronics to social programs like on our website.
    5. Reuse large electronics.

  • What are the first few steps to initiate an e-waste management programme in your apartment complex?

    Form a group with like-minded people. Explain e-waste segregation to your family / neighbours in your apartment building. Get the staff in the apartment building to also understand its importance. repair broken electronics

  • What are the different types of e-waste?

    1. Fridges, freezers and other cooling equipment.

    2. Computers and telecommunications equipment.

    3. Consumer electronic devices and solar panels.

    4. TVs, monitors and screens.

    5. Vending machines.

  • How do I dispose e-waste?

    1. Give Your Electronic Waste to a Certified E-Waste Recycler

    2. Sell Off Your Outdated Technology

    3. Donating Your Outdated Technology

    4. Give Back to Your Electronic Companies and Drop Off Points
